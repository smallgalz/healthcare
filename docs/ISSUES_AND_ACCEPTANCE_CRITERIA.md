# 📋 Issues and Acceptance Criteria for MediChain Contributors

This document outlines the issue types, acceptance criteria, and application process for contributors to participate in the MediChain platform.

## 🎯 Issue Types and Acceptance Criteria

### 1. **EMERGENCY_TREATMENT** 🚨
**Description**: Critical medical emergencies requiring immediate funding

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- ✅ Medical documentation from certified healthcare provider
- ✅ Urgency rating from medical professional
- ✅ Treatment timeline within 7 days
- ✅ Cost breakdown from hospital/clinic
- ✅ Patient consent and privacy compliance

**Funding Range**: $1,000 - $50,000
**Required Approvals**: 3 (Medical, Financial, Community)
**Review Time**: 24-48 hours

---

### 2. **SURGERY** 🏥
**Description**: Planned surgical procedures requiring pre-approval

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- ✅ Surgical recommendation from specialist
- ✅ Hospital cost estimate and breakdown
- ✅ Pre-operative medical clearance
- ✅ Post-operative care plan
- ✅ Insurance coverage details (if any)

**Funding Range**: $5,000 - $100,000
**Required Approvals**: 4 (Medical, Hospital, Financial, Community)
**Review Time**: 3-5 business days

---

### 3. **PREVENTIVE_CARE** 🛡️
**Description**: Preventive medical procedures and screenings

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- ✅ Doctor's recommendation for preventive care
- ✅ Risk assessment documentation
- ✅ Cost-benefit analysis
- ✅ Frequency and schedule details
- ✅ Long-term health impact assessment

**Funding Range**: $500 - $10,000
**Required Approvals**: 2 (Medical, Community)
**Review Time**: 2-3 business days

---

### 4. **CHRONIC_CONDITION** ⏳
**Description**: Ongoing treatment for chronic medical conditions

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- ✅ Chronic condition diagnosis documentation
- ✅ Long-term treatment plan
- ✅ Monthly/quarterly cost breakdown
- ✅ Progress monitoring requirements
- ✅ Alternative treatment options explored

**Funding Range**: $2,000 - $20,000 per year
**Required Approvals**: 3 (Medical, Financial, Community)
**Review Time**: 5-7 business days

---

### 5. **MENTAL_HEALTH** 🧠
**Description**: Mental health treatment and therapy services

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- ✅ Mental health professional diagnosis
- ✅ Treatment plan and duration
- ✅ Therapist/psychologist credentials
- ✅ Confidentiality and privacy plan
- ✅ Progress tracking methodology

**Funding Range**: $1,000 - $15,000
**Required Approvals**: 3 (Mental Health, Financial, Community)
**Review Time**: 3-4 business days

---

### 6. **REHABILITATION** 💪
**Description**: Physical rehabilitation and therapy services

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- ✅ Rehabilitation specialist recommendation
- ✅ Treatment duration and frequency
- ✅ Progress milestones and goals
- ✅ Equipment and facility requirements
- ✅ Expected recovery timeline

**Funding Range**: $3,000 - $25,000
**Required Approvals**: 3 (Medical, Financial, Community)
**Review Time**: 4-5 business days

---

### 7. **MEDICAL_EQUIPMENT** 🏥
**Description**: Medical equipment and assistive devices

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- ✅ Medical prescription for equipment
- ✅ Equipment specifications and costs
- ✅ Vendor quotes and comparisons
- ✅ Maintenance and warranty details
- ✅ Training and support requirements

**Funding Range**: $500 - $20,000
**Required Approvals**: 2 (Medical, Financial)
**Review Time**: 2-3 business days

---

### 8. **RESEARCH_FUNDING** 🔬
**Description**: Medical research and clinical trial participation

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- ✅ Research protocol and IRB approval
- ✅ Institutional affiliation
- ✅ Research timeline and milestones
- ✅ Ethical considerations addressed
- ✅ Potential impact assessment

**Funding Range**: $10,000 - $200,000
**Required Approvals**: 5 (Scientific, Ethical, Financial, Community, Medical)
**Review Time**: 7-14 business days

---

## 📝 Application Process for Contributors

### **Step 1: Contributor Verification**
Contributors must be verified before applying to issues:

**Requirements**:
- ✅ Complete profile with medical credentials (if applicable)
- ✅ Reputation score of at least 50 points
- ✅ Background check and identity verification
- ✅ Agreement to code of ethics and confidentiality

**Levels**:
- **JUNIOR**: 1-5 contributions, 50+ reputation
- **INTERMEDIATE**: 6-15 contributions, 150+ reputation
- **SENIOR**: 16-30 contributions, 300+ reputation
- **EXPERT**: 31-50 contributions, 500+ reputation
- **MASTER**: 51+ contributions, 1000+ reputation

---

### **Step 2: Issue Application**
Contributors apply to specific issues with detailed statements:

**Application Requirements**:
- ✅ Relevant expertise and experience
- ✅ Time commitment availability
- ✅ Specific contribution methodology
- ✅ Expected outcomes and impact
- ✅ Conflict of interest disclosure

**Application Template**:
```
Contributor: [Name/ID]
Issue ID: [Issue Number]
Expertise Area: [Medical Specialty]
Experience: [Years in field]
Time Commitment: [Hours per week]
Approach: [How you'll contribute]
Expected Impact: [Specific outcomes]
Conflicts: [Any conflicts of interest]
```

---

### **Step 3: Review Process**
Applications undergo multi-stage review:

**Review Stages**:
1. **Initial Screening** (24 hours)
   - Completeness check
   - Basic qualification verification
   - Conflict of interest assessment

2. **Expert Review** (2-3 days)
   - Medical expertise validation
   - Experience verification
   - Approach assessment

3. **Community Review** (1-2 days)
   - Community voting
   - Reputation consideration
   - Past performance review

4. **Final Approval** (24 hours)
   - Final decision by approvers
   - Assignment to issue team
   - Onboarding process
